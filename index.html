Privacy Policy

This app is designed for children and does not collect any personal information.

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location, photos, or any other personal data.

The app uses anonymous analytics (PostHog) to understand how features are used, such as which tools are selected. This data is collected in aggregated form and cannot be used to identify any individual user.

The app does not contain advertising, does not track users across apps or websites, and does not share data with third parties for marketing purposes.

All analytics data is used only to improve the app experience.
